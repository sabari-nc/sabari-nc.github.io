Hello!

I am currently a PhD student at the Centre for Disaster Mitigation and Management, Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), Vellore, Tamil Nadu, India. I specialize in monitoring landslides and assessing slope stability in mountainous regions. My research involves applying geophysical techniques, particularly ground pulsation studies, and contributing to mitigation strategies using bioengineering and engineering practices.
